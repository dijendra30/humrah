Community Guidelines

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Version: 1.0

1. Purpose of Humrah

Humrah is a technology platform designed to facilitate in-person meetups between verified adults for public activities such as walks, food outings, events, and exploration. Humrah acts solely as an intermediary service connecting users and does not supervise, control, or guarantee the conduct of users during offline interactions.

Humrah is not a dating application, escort service, or adult entertainment platform. All meetups arranged through Humrah must occur in public places and comply with these Community Guidelines.

2. Eligibility

Use of Humrah is restricted to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. By registering on Humrah, you represent and warrant that you meet this age requirement.

Users under the age of 18 are strictly prohibited from accessing or using the platform. Humrah reserves the right to verify age through documentation and to immediately terminate accounts found to be in violation of this requirement.

3. Public Meetups Only Policy

All meetups arranged through Humrah must take place in public locations such as cafes, parks, restaurants, shopping areas, or public events. Users are prohibited from arranging or suggesting private or residential meetups through the platform.

Humrah strongly recommends that users:

  • Meet only in well-lit, populated public spaces
  • Inform a trusted contact of meetup details
  • Maintain personal safety precautions at all times
  • Trust their instincts and leave any situation that feels unsafe

4. Respect and Harassment Prohibition

All users must treat others with respect and dignity. Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited on Humrah, including but not limited to:

  • Unwanted sexual advances or solicitation
  • Persistent unwanted contact after a user has declined interaction
  • Offensive, degrading, or abusive language
  • Stalking, intimidation, or threatening behavior
  • Sharing of explicit, sexual, or inappropriate content
  • Body shaming, appearance-based insults, or derogatory comments

5. Zero Tolerance Misconduct

Humrah maintains a zero-tolerance policy for the following behaviors, which will result in immediate account termination and potential reporting to law enforcement:

  • Sexual assault, harassment, or misconduct of any nature
  • Physical violence or threats of violence
  • Fraud, scams, or financial exploitation
  • Trafficking, exploitation, or coercion
  • Child endangerment or exploitation
  • Distribution of non-consensual intimate images
  • Drug trafficking or promotion of illegal substances

6. No Sexual Services or Escort Activity

Humrah is not a platform for arranging paid companionship, escort services, or any form of sexual services. Users are strictly prohibited from:

  • Soliciting or offering sexual services in exchange for money, gifts, or other compensation
  • Using the platform to arrange escort services or paid dating
  • Advertising adult services of any nature
  • Engaging in prostitution or solicitation

Violation of this policy will result in immediate permanent ban and reporting to appropriate authorities as required under Indian law, including the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956.

7. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Humrah prohibits hate speech, discrimination, or prejudicial treatment based on:

  • Race, ethnicity, or national origin
  • Religion or religious beliefs
  • Caste or community
  • Gender, gender identity, or sexual orientation
  • Disability or health condition
  • Age
  • Socioeconomic status

Users found promoting hate speech, supremacist ideologies, or discriminatory content will be permanently banned from the platform.

8. No Violence or Threats

Users must not engage in, promote, or threaten violence against any individual or group. This includes:

  • Direct threats of physical harm
  • Incitement to violence
  • Glorification of violent acts
  • Promotion of terrorism or extremist ideologies
  • Self-harm or suicide encouragement

9. No Illegal Activities

Users are prohibited from using Humrah to facilitate, promote, or engage in any illegal activities under Indian law, including but not limited to:

  • Drug trafficking or sale of controlled substances
  • Theft, fraud, or financial crimes
  • Sale of counterfeit goods
  • Unauthorized sale of alcohol or tobacco products
  • Gambling or betting services
  • Money laundering or terrorist financing
  • Hacking, cybercrime, or unauthorized data access

10. No Impersonation or Fake Profiles

All users must provide accurate information and use their real identity on Humrah. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Creating fake or fraudulent profiles
  • Impersonating another person, celebrity, or public figure
  • Using stolen or misleading photographs
  • Misrepresenting age, identity, or location
  • Operating multiple accounts to circumvent bans or restrictions

11. No Misuse of Verification System

Humrah employs video verification and liveness detection to ensure user authenticity. Users must not:

  • Attempt to circumvent or manipulate the verification process
  • Submit verification footage of another person
  • Use deepfake technology, masks, or digital alterations
  • Share verification credentials or verification footage with others
  • Create accounts for third parties

Misuse of the verification system will result in immediate permanent ban and may be reported to law enforcement.

12. No Recording or Sharing Private Conversations

Humrah provides encrypted chat functionality for coordination purposes. Users must respect the privacy of these communications and are prohibited from:

  • Recording, screenshotting, or sharing private conversations without explicit consent
  • Distributing chat logs publicly or to third parties
  • Using conversations for blackmail, extortion, or coercion
  • Sharing intimate or confidential information disclosed in private

13. No Off-Platform Coercion

Users must not use Humrah to pressure, coerce, or manipulate others into moving conversations or interactions to external platforms or private channels for the purpose of evading platform monitoring or engaging in prohibited conduct.

14. Accurate Information Requirement

Users must provide accurate and truthful information in their profiles, including:

  • Real name and accurate age
  • Current and accurate location
  • Authentic photographs that represent current appearance
  • Honest representation of interests and activities

Deliberate misrepresentation constitutes a violation of these Guidelines and may result in account suspension or termination.

15. Reporting Process

Humrah provides a comprehensive reporting system for users to flag violations of these Community Guidelines. Users who experience or witness prohibited conduct should:

  • Use the in-app reporting feature to submit a detailed report
  • Provide any available evidence such as screenshots, chat logs, or incident descriptions
  • Contact Humrah support at support@humrah.in for urgent safety concerns
  • Report serious criminal activity directly to local law enforcement

All reports are reviewed by Humrah's moderation team. Users who submit reports in good faith will not face retaliation. However, false or malicious reporting intended to harass other users will result in enforcement action.

16. Enforcement Actions

Violations of these Community Guidelines may result in the following enforcement actions, at Humrah's sole discretion:

Warning

A formal notice issued for minor or first-time violations. The user's account remains active but is flagged for monitoring.

Strike System

Repeated or moderate violations result in strikes against the user's account. Accumulation of multiple strikes leads to escalated enforcement:

  • First Strike: Warning and temporary account restrictions
  • Second Strike: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
  • Third Strike: Permanent account termination

Temporary Suspension

Account access is temporarily revoked for a defined period. The user cannot log in, access chats, or arrange meetups during suspension.

Permanent Ban

The user's account is permanently terminated. The user is prohibited from creating new accounts on Humrah.

Device-Level Restriction

In cases of severe or repeated violations, Humrah may implement device-level restrictions to prevent the creation of new accounts from the same device.

Humrah reserves the right to take immediate action, including permanent ban without prior warning, for zero-tolerance violations as outlined in Section 5.

17. Appeals Process

Users who believe their account has been suspended or terminated in error may submit an appeal by contacting appeals@humrah.in within 30 days of the enforcement action. Appeals must include:

  • Account email address and user ID
  • Detailed explanation of why the enforcement action should be reconsidered
  • Any supporting evidence or context

Humrah will review appeals in good faith but retains sole discretion in determining final outcomes. Decisions made following the appeals process are final and binding.

18. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Humrah complies with all applicable Indian laws and regulations. In cases involving suspected criminal activity, Humrah reserves the right to:

  • Report incidents to appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Provide user information and communication records as required by lawful requests
  • Cooperate fully with investigations conducted by police or regulatory bodies
  • Preserve evidence and account data related to investigations

This cooperation is conducted in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other applicable Indian legislation.

Humrah reserves the right to modify, update, or revise these Community Guidelines at any time to reflect changes in legal requirements, platform features, or community standards. Users will be notified of material changes through:
