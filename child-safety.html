🎯 1. Our Mission

Humrah is a social companion platform designed exclusively for adults to discover and join verified real-world public activities, aiming to reduce loneliness and foster meaningful connections.

Humrah is NOT a dating app, hookup app, or a platform for minors.

We are deeply committed to creating a safer real-world social experience while maintaining strong moderation and ensuring the absolute safety of all individuals, with a paramount focus on child protection.

🛑 2. Zero Tolerance Policy

Absolute Zero Tolerance

Humrah enforces a strict, absolute zero-tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

Our commitment to this policy involves:

  • Immediate Removal: Any content violating these standards is immediately and permanently removed from our platform.
  • Permanent Account Suspension: Users found uploading, sharing, promoting, or seeking such content will face instantaneous and irreversible account bans.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We actively cooperate with relevant law enforcement agencies, cybercrime units, and non-governmental organizations globally where legally required, reporting severe violations to ensure offenders are held accountable.

🔞 3. Strict 18+ Age Policy

Humrah is strictly built and moderated for adult users. Children are strictly prohibited from creating accounts, accessing content, or using any services provided by Humrah.

Humrah is strictly for users aged 18 years or older. Anyone under the age of 18 is legally and contractually prohibited from using the platform.

  • Account creation requires age declaration and subsequent verification.
  • If the Humrah Safety Team discovers that an account belongs to a minor, or is being operated by someone under 18, it will be permanently suspended and removed immediately.

🚩 4. Reporting System

We empower our community to help maintain a safe environment. Users can instantly flag concerning behavior or content directly within the app.

Users are encouraged and expected to report:

  • Child Exploitation: Any material or discussion exploiting minors.
  • Grooming: Predatory behavior attempting to build trust with minors.
  • Inappropriate Conversations: Solicitations or highly inappropriate language.
  • Sexual Content Involving Minors: Any CSAM or related media.
  • Illegal Activity: Any promotion or coordination of illegal acts.
  • Fake Accounts: Bots or users misrepresenting their identity/age.
  • Harassment & Impersonation: Bullying, stalking, or stealing identities.

All reports are immediately routed to our dedicated Humrah Safety Team for expedited review.

👁️ 5. Moderation & Safety Team

We utilize a multi-layered approach to moderation to ensure that violations are caught swiftly and handled appropriately.

  • Human Moderation: Trained safety specialists manually review flagged content, user reports, and escalated cases to provide nuanced judgment.
  • Proactive Investigations: Our team conducts continuous safety investigations on suspicious patterns and accounts.
  • Immediate Account Review: Accounts flagged for child safety concerns are immediately frozen pending rapid investigation.

⚖️ 6. Enforcement Actions

When a violation of our Child Safety Standards or Community Guidelines is confirmed, Humrah takes immediate and decisive action. Depending on the severity of the violation, actions may include:

  • Warning: Formal notices for minor, non-critical guideline infractions.
  • Temporary Suspension: Restricting access while investigations are ongoing or for repeated minor offenses.
  • Permanent Ban: Lifetime hardware and account bans for severe violations (including any CSAE/CSAM or underage usage).
  • Content Removal: Immediate deletion of offending posts, chats, or profile information.
  • Law Enforcement Escalation: Preserving data and proactively reporting illegal activities involving minors to national and international authorities.

🛡️ 7. Platform Safety Features

Humrah incorporates industry-leading safety tools natively into the application to protect our adult users during their real-world interactions:

  • Profile Verification: Secure account verification processes to ensure real, adult identities.
  • User Blocking & Reporting: One-tap access to block unwanted contact and report malicious users.
  • Emergency SOS: In-app panic buttons for immediate assistance during real-world meetups.
  • Live Location Sharing: User-initiated, encrypted location sharing with trusted contacts during public activities.
  • Clear Guidelines: Enforced Community Guidelines accessible to all users.

📜 8. Compliance Statement

Humrah is fully committed to operating within the highest legal and ethical frameworks globally.

We officially comply with:

  • Google Play Child Safety Standards for social and communication applications.
  • Applicable Indian Laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and POCSO Act guidelines for digital platforms.
  • International Child Protection Requirements for digital service providers.
