Delete Your Account

📅 Last Updated: February 16, 2026  ·  📌 Account Management

Please read carefully before proceeding

This page explains how you can permanently delete your Humrah account and all associated personal data. Account deletion is final and cannot be reversed. Please read all the information below before making this decision.

⚠️

Important Notice — This Action Cannot Be Undone

  • Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be reversed under any circumstances.
  • All your personal data, profile, matches, and conversations will be permanently deleted.
  • You will lose access to all your connections, bookings, and activity history.
  • You cannot recover your account once deletion has been confirmed.
📱

Method 1 — Delete via the App

Recommended · Fastest way to delete your account

1

Open the Humrah app

Launch the app on your device and ensure you are logged into the account you wish to delete.

2

Go to Profile → Scroll down

Tap on your Profile icon, then scroll to the bottom of the Profile page.

3

Tap "Delete Account"

Find and tap the "Delete Account" option at the very bottom of the Profile page.

4

Read the warning message

A warning screen will appear outlining everything that will be permanently deleted. Review it carefully before proceeding.

5

Confirm deletion using your password

Enter your account password to verify your identity, then tap "Confirm Delete" to complete the request.

ℹ️ Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion and immediately deactivated. You will be logged out automatically and will no longer be visible to other users.
✉️

Method 2 — Request Deletion via Email

Use this if you cannot access the app

1

Send an email to support@humrah.in

Compose an email from your registered email address to support@humrah.in.

2

Use the subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Set the email subject line exactly as: Account Deletion Request

3

Include your registered email or phone number

In the email body, state the email address or phone number linked to your Humrah account.

4

Include your name for verification

Provide your full name as registered on Humrah to assist with identity verification.

🔐 We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. This step ensures your account is only deleted at your explicit request.
🗑️
