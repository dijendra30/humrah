Account Management

Delete Your Account

This page explains how you can permanently delete your Humrah account and all associated personal data. Please read carefully before proceeding.

⚠️

This action cannot be undone

  • Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible.
  • All your personal data, profile, matches, and conversations will be permanently deleted.
  • You will not be able to recover your account or any data after deletion is confirmed.
📱

Method 1: Delete via the App

Recommended — fastest way to delete your account

1

Open the Humrah app

Launch the app on your device and make sure you are logged in.

2

Go to Profile → Settings

Tap on your Profile icon, then scroll down to the bottom of the Profile page.

3

Tap "Delete Account"

Find and tap the "Delete Account" option listed at the bottom of the page.

4

Read the warning message

A warning screen will appear outlining what will be permanently deleted. Review it carefully.

5

Confirm with your password

Enter your account password to confirm the deletion request and tap "Confirm Delete".

ℹ️ Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion and deactivated immediately. You will be logged out automatically.
✉️

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

Use this if you cannot access the app

1

Compose an email

Send an email to support@humrah.in from your registered email address.

2

Use the correct subject line

Set the email subject as exactly: "Account Deletion Request"

3

Include your account details

In the email body, provide your registered email address or phone number linked to your Humrah account.

4

Include your name

Include your full name as registered on Humrah so we can verify your identity.

🔐 We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing the deletion. This is to ensure your account is only deleted at your request.
🗑️

What Gets Deleted

All associated personal data is permanently removed

👤 Account profile
🖼️ Photos & media
💬 Messages & chats
❤️ Matches & connections
⚙️ Preferences & settings
📊 Activity & usage data
📍 Location history
📋 Stored personal information
🛡️ We do not retain any personally identifiable information after deletion, except where required by applicable law or for legitimate security and fraud prevention purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
🕐

Deletion Timeline

What happens after you confirm deletion

⏱️

Within 24 hours

Account deactivated

Your account is immediately deactivated and becomes invisible to other users. You will no longer be able to log in.

✅

Within 7 days

Data permanently deleted

All your personal data, profile information, messages, and associated records are permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.
