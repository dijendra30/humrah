4. Face Verification & Biometric Data

Critical Information: Humrah uses facial recognition technology for security purposes. Please read this section carefully.

What We Collect

When you complete video verification:

  • Short Video Recording: Brief video performing liveness detection
  • Face Embeddings: Mathematical representations of facial features

How Face Verification Works

  1. You record a short verification video
  2. System analyzes video to confirm you're a real person
  3. Unique face embedding is generated
  4. Embedding compared against database to detect duplicates
  5. Original video deleted within 48 hours

What Are Face Embeddings?

Face embeddings are NOT images. They are:

  • Mathematical representations (arrays of numbers)
  • Irreversible â€“ cannot be converted back to an image
  • Used solely for comparing similarity between faces
  • Stored in encrypted format

Why We Use This Technology

  • Safety: Prevents banned users from creating new accounts
  • Authenticity: Ensures users are real people
  • Trust: Builds confidence in the community
  • Fraud Prevention: Detects platform abuse

Important: Face verification is mandatory to use Humrah. Your face embedding will be permanently deleted when you delete your account.

5. Location Data

Types of Location Data

Approximate Location (Required)

  • What: City-level or neighborhood-level location
  • How: Derived from IP address or coarse permission
  • Why: To match you with nearby companions
  • Precision: Not precise enough to identify exact address

Precise Location (Optional)

  • What: GPS coordinates when using location features
  • How: Only when you grant precise location permission
  • Why: To help find nearby public places
  • Control: Revoke in device settings anytime

How We Use Location Data

  • Match you with nearby users
  • Suggest public places for meetups
  • Show distance estimates
  • Prevent misuse by detecting unusual patterns

We Never: Track your location continuously in the background, share your precise location publicly, or use location data for advertising.

7. Data Retention & Storage

Active Account Data

  • Profile Information: Retained while account is active
  • Face Embeddings: Retained for security purposes
  • Chat Messages: Retained for 90 days
  • Booking History: Retained for 1 year

Deleted Account Data

When you delete your account:

  • Immediate: Profile photos, profile info, chat messages
  • 30 Days: Face embeddings
  • 90 Days: Email address and device identifiers

Temporary Data

  • Verification Videos: Deleted within 48 hours
  • Log Files: Deleted after 180 days
  • Session Data: Deleted on logout or after 30 days

8. Your Privacy Rights

Under Indian data protection law, you have the following rights:

Right to Object

How to Exercise Your Rights

11. Children's Privacy

Parental Rights

If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has created an account, email us immediately at support@humrah.in with proof of guardianship.
